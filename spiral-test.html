Spiral wipe tuner

The level-complete number spiral, running the real in-game renderer. Drag the sliders to find a speed and turn count you like.

In-game: 2000 ms, 2.5 turns. Grab the scrub slider to freeze a phase; hit Auto to release. Set chosen values in WIN_WIPE_MS (src/game/config.js) and the turns default in renderSpiralWipe (src/render/render.js).
